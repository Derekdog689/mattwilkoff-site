Recovery-Through-Music

Workshops & small groups that use songwriting and voice to rebuild confidence and connection.

  • Small-group workshop (6–12 participants)
  • Program visits / facility partnerships

Focus Areas

Lyric journaling • values → verses • sharing safely
Breath & voice • basic rhythm • simple recording

*MR Studios is not a clinical provider. Workshops complement — not replace — therapy or treatment.
